# Workshop Overview
This workshop prepares you for your final project presentation, teaching you how to effectively showcase your team's accomplishments within a 5-6 minute timeframe. Learn how to structure your presentation, design engaging slides, and deliver with confidence.

## What You'll Learn
- Presentation structure and timing
- Effective slide design principles
- Public speaking techniques
- Demo preparation strategies
- Audience engagement methods
- Team presentation coordination

## Prerequisites
- Project near completion
- Basic understanding of presentation tools
- Familiarity with your project's key features

## Workshop Structure
1. Introduction (15 minutes)
   - Presentation goals and expectations
   - Time management
   - Audience considerations

2. Presentation Preparation (30 minutes)
   - Content organization
   - Key message identification
   - Story development
   - Team role assignment

3. Slide Design (30 minutes)
   - Visual hierarchy principles
   - Content-to-visual ratio
   - Brand consistency
   - Typography and color usage
   - Image and graphic selection

4. Presentation Outline (30 minutes)
   - Introduction and team overview
   - Problem statement
   - Solution demonstration
   - Technical highlights
   - Future developments
   - Q&A preparation

5. Delivery Techniques (15 minutes)
   - Speaking confidence
   - Body language
   - Timing management
   - Team transitions
   - Technical demonstration tips

## Expected Outcomes
By the end of this workshop, participants will be able to:
- Structure a compelling 5-6 minute presentation
- Create visually engaging slides
- Coordinate team presentation roles
- Deliver with confidence
- Handle Q&A effectively

## General Information
- Presentations occur during the first week of December
- 5-6 minute time limit
- Audience includes cohort members, guests, and industry partners
- Focus on demonstrating project accomplishments

## Key Components

### Preparation
- Rehearse multiple times
- Time each section
- Prepare backup plans for demos
- Test all technical elements
- Practice smooth transitions

### Outline Structure
- Team introduction (30 seconds)
- Problem statement (1 minute)
- Solution overview (2 minutes)
- Technical highlights (1.5 minutes)
- Future vision (30 seconds)
- Q&A readiness

### Slide Design Tips
- Keep text minimal
- Use high-quality visuals
- Maintain consistent branding
- Include clear demonstrations
- Focus on key achievements
