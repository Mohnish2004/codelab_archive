# Workshop Overview

Our DI x Codelab collaboration featured a deep dive into the world of cross-functional teamwork with Industry and freelance software engineers and designers from companies like Deloitte, Coinbase, and C3 AI. They shared valuable insights on how they collaborate effectively to build seamless and user-centric products.

A huge thank you to our incredible speakers Christina Liu, Divya Gautam, Keith Low, and Sauvikesh Lal, and everyone who joined us for this insightful session! Stay tuned for more events where we continue exploring the intersections of design and engineering.

## Key Takeaways

- Understanding the different mindsets and priorities of designers and developers
- Effective communication strategies for bridging the gap between design and engineering
- Best practices for design handoff and documentation
- Navigating technical constraints and feasibility considerations
- Real-world workflows and collaboration tools that enhance efficiency

## Workshop Structure

1. Opening Discussion
- The importance of cross-functional collaboration in modern product development
- Common challenges faced when working across disciplines

2. Industry Speaker Insights
- How designers and engineers align on vision and execution
- Strategies for reducing friction in the development process

3. Best Practices for Collaboration
- Tools and workflows that streamline teamwork
- Balancing creativity with technical feasibility

4. Q&A Session
- Attendees engaged in a lively discussion, asking about industry trends, career advice, and overcoming collaboration challenges

## Event slides and key takeaways

By the end of this workshop, participants will be able to:
- Understand industry workflows in design and development
- How designers and developers balance feasibility, creativity, and collaborating cross-functionally
- Hear candid insights transitioning from university to industry
