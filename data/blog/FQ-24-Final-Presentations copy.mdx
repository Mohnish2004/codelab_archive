## About CodeLab

Founded in 2020 at UC Davis, CodeLab has grown from an initial team of 7 to over 200 members, becoming one of the largest software development and design agencies on campus. Our mission is to provide students with hands-on software development experience through real-world projects and mentorship, bridging the gap between academic learning and industry practices.

### Our Impact by the Numbers
- **60+** Projects Completed
- **200+** Active Members
- **3** Core Focus Areas: Software Engineering, UX Design, and Project Management

### What We Deliver
CodeLab specializes in quarter-long Computer Science and design projects, including:
- Company dashboards
- Internal tooling
- Software solutions
- UI/UX improvements

### Our Approach
We focus on three key areas:
1. Gaining hands-on real-world experience
2. Working on cross-functional teams
3. Building large-scale projects

## Looking Forward

For Winter/Spring 2024, we're actively seeking new partnerships with innovative companies in the tech space. Our teams bring fresh perspectives, technical expertise, and dedicated effort to every project we undertake.
